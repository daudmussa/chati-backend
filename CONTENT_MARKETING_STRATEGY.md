## âœï¸ Writing Tips for Blog Posts

### Structure:
1. **Catchy Title** with numbers or how-to
2. **Introduction**: Problem + solution preview
3. **Body**: Clear sections with H2/H3 headings

### Style Guidelines:
- Simple language (8th-grade reading level)
- Short paragraphs (3-4 sentences)
- Bullet points for scannability
- Active voice
- Include numbers and data
- Tell stories, not just features
